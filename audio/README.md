## Recommendations:
- Format: MP3
- Bitrate: 128kbps (good balance between quality and file size)
- Duration: 2-4 minutes per track
- Total size: Keep under 20MB for all tracks

## Tips:
- Use online converters or Audacity to convert to 128kbps MP3
- Choose songs that are meaningful to your relationship
- Consider using instrumental versions if you want focus on stats
- Test audio playback locally before deploying
